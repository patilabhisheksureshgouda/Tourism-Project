jodphur

Day 3 : Jaipur to Jodhpur | Home to the Impregnable Mehrangarh Fort

Enjoy a delicious breakfast in the morning and then get ready to transfer to Jodhpur. On reaching the hotel, complete the check-in process and take some rest. Once ready, you can proceed to explore the stunning blue city of Jodhpur.

Later in the evening, return back to the hotel in Jodhpur for an overnight stay.

Jaisalmer

Day 4 : Jodhpur to Jaisalmer | Travel to the Shimmering 'Golden City' of Rajasthan

Start your next day with a delicious breakfast then get ready to transfer to Jaisalmer, also known as the Golden City. Take a short stopover at the Jaisalmer War Memorial, which honors the contributions and sacrifices of war heroes, and also traces the evolution of the Indian Army.

Day 5 : Sightseeing in Jaisalmer | Explore the Secrets Hidden Behind the Beautiful Sand Dunes

After breakfast, you can proceed to uncover the secrets hidden behind the Golden Sands of the Thar Desert in Jaisalmer. You can visit the golden Jaisalmer Fort that looks like it has just risen from the sands with its golden limestone walls.
