Basilica of Bom Jesus

Day 1 :start with goa.

Arrival Upon reaching Goa, you can check-in to your hotel, and either opt to spend your day relaxing, or head out to explore the nearby markets.

After having your breakfast, you can set off to North Goa, and enjoy some remarkable sightseeing tours of historical churches and forts.

Arambol Beach

Day 02 : Explore the culture of Goa.

Apart from the beaches of Goa, North Goa has a cultural side too that the visitors can check out to feel the real essence of the place.

Day 03 : you can also visit some other secrete falls.

Old & South Goa

Day 4 : Sightseeing in Old & South Goa.

After breakfast, start your day by exploring the traditional roots of Old Goa and check out Basilica Bom Jesus Church and Se Cathedral.

Then, you can rent a bike or a car to visit the most popular beach in South Goa.

Dudhsagar Falls

Day 5 : Visit the Dudhsagar Falls.

While you must be thinking that North Goa is home to the best beaches, then you are forgetting the Dudhsagar Falls, which should be one of the highlights of your trip.

After this, you can visit the Aguada Fort, which lies within one-and-half hours by road from the falls.

Mapusa market

Day 6 : Visit the mapusa market

A famous market and fondly known as 'Mapusa Friday Market' as it is taken place every Friday.

ou'll find all kind of requirements from lottery stalls to barbers shops, fruits stalls to vegetable stalls, there is fish street as well as dry fish stalls with all kinds of fish.
