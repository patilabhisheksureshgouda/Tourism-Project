JAMMU & KASHMIR

Srinagar - Pahalgam

Day 1 : start with Pahalgam.

Meet and greet on arrival at Srinagar Airport and proceed to Pahalgam (3-4 hrs). Check into the hotel. Rest of the day free for exploring local surroundings. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Meals & Drinks : Dinner

Gulmarg

Day 02 : Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar.

Early morning after breakfast proceed for Gulmaeg (2/3 hrs). Enjoy Gandola cable car Phase I. Later noon explore local surroundings (by walk or on pony). Back to Srinagar by the evening. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Day 03 : Today visit famous Mughal gardens like Shalimar baug, Nishat baug, Chashmeshahi baug. Also visit Shankaracharya Temple

Meals & Drinks : Dinner

kargil-leh

Day 4 : Srinagar - Kargil - leh

After breakfast leave for Leh (8-9 hrs) by visiting Mulbek (1st Century Giant Living Buddha Sculpture on Rock Mountain), Lamayuru Monastery, Moonland, Magnetic Hill, Confluence of Indus and Zanskar rivers and Pathar saheb Gurudwara. Reach Leh and check into the hotel. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Meals & Drinks : Breakfast, Dinner

Leh

Day 5 : visit a leh.

After breakfast visit Leh City sightseeing which includes Hemis Monastery - Oldest and richest monastery of Ladakh, Shey palace, Sindhu ghat, Hall of fame museum, Shanti stupa and Leh Palace. Evening free to explore Leh market

Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Hotspots : Leh Palace

Meals & Drinks : Breakfast, Dinner

Ladakh

Day 6Ladakh is one place in India which remains on almost every travellers bucket list and is a dream destination which will remain etched in his mind even years after the trip is over! Ladakhs majestic and raw beauty is one which captivates every tourist visiting this place while also leaving them longing for more.

Dinner and overnight at hotel.
